Hi, my name is Viktoria Bellak. I'm a UX Designer from Vienna, AT.

Having my roots in project management and being a Scrum Master for many years while also doing plenty of Design Thinking, it was only logical to continue the path with UX Design. I feel most comfortable in the area of User Research and love bringing ideas to life and turning them into something interactive for users to try out, to keep improving my projects steadily. On this page you can find an overview of my work.

My Work

VELA

During a 6-month project I created an application specifically designed for sailors: VELA - Your one in all solution for the most enjoyable sailing experience! For this project I went from conducting the competitive analysis, to creating interview guidelines, moderating user interviews and processing the outcomes to sketching out with pen and paper before transferring my visions into an interactive, clicking prototype using Figma and conducting usability testing to improve the prototype.

View Project Details

Linguino

My first project was Linguino: a fun vocabulary learning application that's supposed to help users reach their personal language learning goals. In the iterative creation process for Linguino a competitive analysis kicked off the research phase, followed by user interviews, defining personas and setting up the information architecture of the app. After creating a low-fidelity prototype with pen and paper, three user tests were conducted to gather feedback and polish the prototype.
